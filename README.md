Classic Models Sales Dashboard
Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance using the Classic Models database and presents key business insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard. The dashboard enables users to explore sales trends, product performance, customer distribution, and regional performance to support data-driven decision-making.

Business Problem

Classic Models is a retailer of scale model vehicles. The company requires a centralized reporting solution to monitor sales performance, identify top-performing products, and better understand customer purchasing behavior.

Project Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance.
Identify top-performing product lines.
Compare sales across countries and offices.
Monitor customer purchasing patterns.
Develop an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

Key Insights
Product Performance
Classic Cars generated the highest revenue at approximately $3.9M.
Vintage Cars generated approximately $1.8M in sales.
Motorcycles, Trucks & Buses, and Planes showed moderate performance.
Trains generated the lowest revenue among all product categories.
Geographic Performance
The USA was the highest-performing market.
France and Australia contributed significantly to overall sales.
Sales were distributed across multiple international markets.
Sales Trends
Sales increased significantly during the later periods of the dataset.
Revenue growth was accompanied by increased order volume.
Average order values remained relatively stable.
Business Recommendations
Increase focus on high-performing product lines such as Classic Cars and Vintage Cars.
Investigate factors affecting low-performing product categories.
Strengthen customer retention strategies for high-value customers.
Explore opportunities to increase sales in underperforming regions.
